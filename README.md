
Proposed Solution: Agentic AI for Mental Health Awareness and Suicide Prevention
The proposed solution is a multi-agent AI-powered Mental Health Awareness and Suicide Prevention System built using LangFlow, IBM watsonx.ai, and Granite Models to provide personalized, proactive, and real-time mental health support. The system addresses critical challenges in mental health awareness, emotional well-being monitoring, early suicide risk detection, and timely intervention through an intelligent, modular architecture.
At its core, the solution utilizes a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline to access reliable mental health knowledge from trusted sources such as the World Health Organization (WHO), National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH), Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), and validated psychological wellness resources. This information is embedded and stored in a vector database, enabling the system to retrieve and generate accurate, context-aware responses related to mental health conditions, coping mechanisms, crisis support, and preventive care using Granite models.
The system consists of four key agents:
1. Mental Health Knowledge Agent
Retrieves and summarizes evidence-based mental health information using RAG. 
2. Emotional Assessment Agent
Detects emotional states such as stress, anxiety, loneliness, hopelessness, sadness, or emotional distress. 
Continuously monitors behavioral and emotional patterns to identify early warning signs. 
3. Personalized Support & Recommendation Agent
Generates personalized wellness plans based on user emotional status, age group, lifestyle, and mental health needs. 
4. Suicide Risk Detection & Crisis Intervention Agent
Performs risk assessment and classifies users into Low, Moderate, High, or Critical risk categories. 
LangFlow is used to orchestrate agent workflows and manage inter-agent communication, while IBM watsonx.ai provides scalable model deployment, embeddings, governance, security, and responsible AI capabilities. Granite Models power advanced reasoning, sentiment understanding, emotional analysis, summarization, and personalized support generation.
